Data Protection

Privacy policy and information about how we handle personal data (demo project).

1. Overview

This website is a demonstration project created for an academic course. It is not an official website of a real organization. Any forms, bookings, or payments shown on this website are simulated and do not result in real transactions.

3. What Data We Process

Depending on how you use the website, we may process the following categories of data:

  • Technical data (e.g., browser type, operating system, time of access).
  • Log data (e.g., IP address in server logs, if enabled by the hosting environment).
  • Data you provide in forms (e.g., name and email address), if you submit a form.

4. Purpose and Legal Basis

We process personal data only to operate this website and to respond to inquiries. Where applicable, the legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in operating a secure website) and/or Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract or pre-contractual measures) for simulated booking/contact flows.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

This website may use cookies or local storage to remember language settings or user inputs for usability reasons. You can delete cookies and website data at any time in your browser settings.

6. Third-Party Services

This demo project is designed to work without third-party tracking. If external services (e.g., fonts, maps, embedded media) are added later, they may process personal data (such as IP address) when loaded. In that case, this policy should be updated accordingly.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the stated purposes. In a demo context, form submissions are not intended to be stored permanently.

8. Your Rights

If personal data is processed, you may have rights under the GDPR, including the right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and the right to object. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect this website. However, data transmission over the internet (e.g., email) can have security gaps. Complete protection of data against access by third parties is not possible.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes to this demo website. The current version is published on this page.
