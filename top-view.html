The Top View

According to visitors and organizers, the top view of Rosenheim is the most compelling attraction. It represents a marvelous bird’s-eye view of the beautifully organized city.

Most visitors come from Rosenheim and nearby towns. Many are used to living in one- to two-storey houses, so viewing the city from a height comparable to ten storeys offers a completely new perspective.

On clear sunny days, the nearby Alps are often visible without any devices. Since there are no taller buildings nearby, the view opens up widely and helps broaden the mind and thoughts.

As mentioned in the Tower Tour section, the climb can be a bit challenging, with steep and narrow stairs. For many sport-loving visitors, this is part of the fun. After climbing ten floors, blood circulation improves, which helps sharpen the senses. This makes the wide view even more enjoyable. At this height, the air feels fresher and cleaner. Together, these effects create a memorable experience that many visitors would happily repeat. It is also a perfect place to take spectacular photos and surprise friends and family.

What makes the top view special

Alps on clear days

On a clear sunny day, the Alps can be visible in the distance, making the view feel expansive and memorable.

Rooftops & city layout

Many houses have colored roofs and one-storey structures. This clearly organized and beautiful cityscape is a sight to behold from the top.

Fresh air & reward

After the climb, the open air at the top feels like a reward and refreshes the senses.

Perfect for photos

A great place to take spectacular, unique photos and share with friends and family.

“For many visitors, this is the most compelling attraction of the tour.”

— Visitors and organizers

After climbing through all floors and experiencing many attractions, the open and fresh top area feels truly rewarding. With sharper senses and fresh air, visitors often want to return again.
